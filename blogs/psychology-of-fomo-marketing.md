Have you ever rushed to buy a plane ticket because the site said "Only 2 seats left at this price"? That is **Scarcity Marketing** in action. It triggers a psychological response known as **Loss Aversion**—the pain of losing out is psychologically twice as powerful as the pleasure of gaining.

### The "Low Stock" Trigger

Displaying a real-time inventory counter (e.g., "Only 3 items in stock") forces the brain to make a decision now rather than later.

**The Data:** Real-time social proof notifications have been shown to boost conversions by up to 98%.

![How scarcity marketing funnel works](/v1/blogs/fomo-funnel.png)

### The "Social Validation" Loop

When a visitor sees "Someone in London just purchased this," it validates their own desire. It provides safety in numbers. This is why apps like FOMO Gen combine stock counters with sales notifications—it hits both the "Urgency" and "Trust" triggers simultaneously.
