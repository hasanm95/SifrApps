I used to wake up every morning with a big goal: I wanted to build something great for the AppLass ecosystem. But by 10:00 AM, I would usually find myself stuck in a "dopamine loop," scrolling through feeds I didn't even care about.

I realized I was trying to fight a lopsided battle—my brain was running "legacy code" (my own willpower), while social media apps were running super-computer algorithms designed to win.

**The cost wasn't just "wasted time"; it was the loss of roughly 2.4 hours every single day** that I could have spent on my apps.

## The Broken Promise of "Focus"

Whether you are a student trying to study, a developer trying to code, or a business owner trying to grow, the problem is the same: **your focus is a system resource that is constantly being attacked.**

- **The Job Holder:** Even one notification can kill your "flow" for 23 minutes.
- **The Student:** It is nearly impossible to learn complex math or engineering when your brain is fragmented by micro-interruptions.
- **The Business Owner:** You can't make smart decisions when you are suffering from decision fatigue caused by "doomscrolling."

## I Tried the "Soft" Solutions (They Failed)

I spent months trying apps like Opal and Freedom, but they never felt right:

- Some were **way too expensive** for what they offered
- Others wanted to route all my private data through their servers using a VPN
- Worst of all, most of them were "soft"—they let me click "5 more minutes" and bypass my own goals

As a developer, I knew I needed something built with **mathematical precision** and **zero-telemetry privacy**.
