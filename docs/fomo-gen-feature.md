# Complete Features List

## Core Social Proof Features

### Real-Time Purchase Notifications

- Show pop-up notifications when someone makes a purchase
- Display what product was bought and when
- Show customer's first name and city (with privacy controls)
- Use real order data from your Shopify store

### Live Visitor Activity

- Show how many people are currently viewing products
- Display when someone adds items to their cart
- Show recent product views and browsing activity

### Social Proof Badges

- "X people bought this today" counters
- "X items sold in the last 24 hours" badges
- "Trending product" labels based on real sales data
- "Most popular" tags for best-selling items

## Urgency & Scarcity Features

### Stock Level Notifications

- Show "Low stock" warnings when inventory gets low
- Display exact quantity left ("Only 3 remaining!")
- "Selling fast" badges for high-demand products
- Automatic restock notifications

### Countdown Timers

- Sale countdowns for store-wide promotions
- Product launch countdowns
- Limited-time offer timers
- Cart abandonment countdowns ("Offer expires in 10:00")

### Progress Indicators (Cart-Based)

- Free shipping progress bar showing how close customer is to free shipping
- Goal progress trackers ("Spend $15 more to reach your goal!")
- Real-time progress updates as customers add items to cart
- Customizable thresholds, messages, and colors
- Display on cart, product pages, or store-wide

### Stock Alerts (Product-Specific)

- Low stock alerts for individual products ("Only 5 left in stock!")
- Real-time inventory tracking from Shopify
- Automatic display when inventory falls below threshold
- Out-of-stock warnings when products are sold out
- Product-specific urgency messaging
- Shows on product pages and cart (when product is in cart)
- Customizable stock thresholds per product
- Urgency-focused styling (orange/red colors)

## Display & Customization

### Notification Styles

- Multiple position options (bottom corner, top banner, inline)
- Different animation styles (slide-in, fade, bounce)
- Custom colors to match your store's branding
- Mobile-optimized responsive designs

### Display Rules

- Control which pages show notifications (homepage, product pages, collection pages)
- Set how often notifications appear
- Delay first notification until customer has been browsing for a while
- Hide on checkout and cart pages

### Targeting Options

- Show different messages to new vs. returning customers
- Geographic targeting by country or city
- Device-specific rules (mobile vs. desktop)
- Traffic source targeting (social media, email, search)

## Smart Automation

### Smart Timing

- Quiet hours - don't show notifications during off-hours
- Frequency capping - don't overwhelm customers
- Session-based limits - max notifications per visit

### Behavior-Based Triggers

- Show notifications when customer is about to leave
- Trigger messages after customer views a product
- Show cross-sell notifications based on browsing history
- Abandoned cart reminders

### Inventory-Smart Rules

- Only show low-stock notifications for actual low-stock items
- Automatically adjust messaging based on sales velocity
- Hide notifications for out-of-stock products
